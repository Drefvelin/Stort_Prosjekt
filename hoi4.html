Hearts of Iron IV is a game developed by Paradox Interactive. The main point of the game is to play throught a World War 2 scenario, from 1936 to 1945. You can choose between any country present at either the 1936 start, or the 1939 start.

The game can seem very complex and is hard to understand fully as a beginner. Therefore i have created this guide which will help you on the road to World Conquest. You can choose what you want to learn about from the 3 main categories. Military, National Focus and Research.
