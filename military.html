Military in Hearts of Iron IV

Your most important asset on the road to World Conquest is your military might. In Hearts of Iron IV there are 3 main branches of the military, the Army, the Navy and the Airforce. To master all three would significantly improve your skill level and help you conwuer nations more easily

The Military in this game might seem vey confusing at first, which is why there is a page dedicated to each branch of the military on this website.

Using the pages below you can improve your understanding of using things like tanks CAS or Submarines. Your tactics depend entirely on what you want to achieve and these pages are meerly a guide to help you on your way to finding your own strategy.
