Research in HOI4

Hearts of Iron IV spans over several years, and during WW2 in history, technology developed a lot. Therefore it is very important to stay on top of the game of technology and research to ensure you are not outmatched by the enemy!

There are a very large amount of different technologies you can choose from in the game. The technologies will help you and ease your path in almost every part of the game, from army to navy to industry. How advanced your nation is often depends on what nation you start as.

Army Doctrine

The Army Doctrine is what you research to improve your army capabilities. If you want defeat the enemy in classic land battles you MUST have a good doctrine. The doctrines are as follows: Mobile Warfare, Superior Firepower, Grand Battleplan and Mass Assault. They are useful for different things. MW is good for tanks, SF is good for artillery, GB is good for defense and MA is good for infantry and manpower.

Navy Doctrine

For your navy to operate efficiently and to win battles you cannot solely rely on having the better ships, you also have to have a good doctrine to give your ships buffs. The doctrines are as follows: Fleet in Being, Trade Interdiction and Base Strike. FiB is good for having a large "f*ck you" fleet that destroyes anyone, TI is good for smaller, more concealed(but effective) fleets and BS is good for carrier operations (The Pacific)

Air Doctrine

Air Doctrines are very useful in conservint your planes, defeating enemy planes and overall efficiency when taking the skies. The doctrines are as follows: Strategic Destruction, Battlefield Support and Operational Integrity. SD is very good for strategic bombers, BS is good for CAS and fighters and OI is good for tactical bombers and fighters.

The Infantry Weapons Branch helps you develop new weapons, vehicles and elite forces to improve you roverall ability to fight land wars. Using these techs properly will give you a good advantage when facing an enemy with inferior tech.

The Support Tech branch gives you the ability to add support companies to your divisions. Support companies are almost essential in any non-garrison division. they cost very little to add but can make a huge difference when fighting in a a battle.

The Armor tech branch will, you guessed it, unlock new tanks. This is how you reserach the tanks you can use to make the divisions on the previous Army page. Tanks are split into Amphibious, Light, Medium, Heavy and Modern Tanks. The faster you get to Modern Tanks the better.

The Artillery Branch unlocks the ability to add artillery brigades to you divisions, these are offensive brigades, they add little defense, but have very good soft attack. It is a must in infantry divisions meant for attack. This branch also allows you to research Anti-Tank, Anti-Air and Rocket Artillery.

The Navy Tech branch allows you to research new ship hulls and modules that you can use in the ship designer to engineer and customize the perfect ship for your fleet. This page is also where you find the transport ship techs needed to do naval invasions.

The Air Tech branch allows you to research new aircraft you can start producing. It is split between CAS, Fighters, Naval Bombers, Heavy Fighters, Tactical Bombers and Strategic Bombers. Using bonuses you can get from your National Focus you can quickly research the most modern planes.

The Electronics Branch is a branch you use for more technical and "nitpicky" techs. You can for example develop RADAR technology, Decryption and Encryption Methods, Nukes and overall improve the speed at which you research.

The Industry tech branch is one ofT THE most important tech branches. any Industry would be completely crippled unless you research these technologies. Here you can improve the efficiency of your factories, Oil refining and max factories per state.
